# Ethics and Human Participant Considerations

## Human Participant Status
This project involves **one human participant**, who is also the student researcher.

Under ISEF guidelines, self-experimentation is still considered human participant
research.

---

## Risk Assessment
- Non-invasive EEG recording
- Normal listening volume
- No physical, psychological, or medical intervention
- Minimal risk

---

## Oversight
This project is submitted for **IRB/SRC review and approval prior to data collection**
in accordance with ISEF Human Participants rules.

If the participant is under 18 years of age:
- Parental permission
- Student assent  
are obtained as required.
